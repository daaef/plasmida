Entrepreneurship and Skill Acquisition Trainings

Since inception of office, His Excellency, Governor Simon Bako Lalong established the Micro Finance & SME’s Agency of Plateau state which is saddled with the responsibility of promotion of Financial Literacy, Entrepreneurship, Vocational and Skill Acquisition. This led to the initiation of Youth and Women Empowerment Programmes among others. Immediately, the Simon Lalong Empowerment Scheme (SLEMS) was launched to reduce poverty of the Plateau citizens. Specifically, on the 9th of March, 2017. Over 200 inputs were donated to the best three graduating participants’ across the various skills in all seventeen Local Government Areas; the skill sets included; (Fashion Design, Leather Works, Hair Dressing, Computer, Weaving, and Catering Services). The participant included people living with disability, women and youths and the components of the training included Entrepreneurship, Financial Literacy and Skill Acquisition with over 3000 participants in all.

WOMEN SKILL EMPOWERMENT PROGRAMME (WOSEP) IN PARTNERSHIP WITH THE INDUSTRIAL TRAINING FUND.

The Agency in it numerous thrive also has gone into partner with the Industrial Training Fund (ITF) on its various project. One of which is the Women Skill Empowerment Programme (WOSEP), is an initiative of the Industrial Training Fund (ITF) to key into the President Mohammadu Buhari’s policy of Job and Wealth Creation, inclusive growth and sustainable development in order to address the massive unemployment problem and similar challenges facing the country. WOSEP is captured under the Quick Wins Strategy of the Industrial Training Fund considering that the programme was to equip vulnerable women with requisite skills for setting up and managing small scale businesses as well as make them employable. The training which lasted a period of three month started in May 2017 and after the training, a ceremony conducted mark the end of the training and the event at took place at the Women Multipurpose Center,Jos.

SLEMS – PEOPLE LIVING WITH DISABILITY AND YOUTH EMPOWERMENT PROGRAMME:

On the 13th of July, 2017 the flag-off of the entrepreneurship development training programme for People Living with Disability and Youth, the Agency initiated the mobilization and documentation for the second phase of the SLEMS initiative. Over 3400 participant have been mobilized, with arrangement to initiate seed funding for the participants after the training as plans are being concluded as participants are currently undergoing training in the following skill sets: Poultry Farming, Cosmetology, Leather Works, Food and Beverages and POP and Paint Making and also Barbing and Hair Styling.

SLEM – PASTORALIST TRAINING

Under the SLEMS initiative, another programme was flagged off. This is the Pastoralist Training for youths on the Plateau. The programme was flagged of on the 13th of July, 2017 and had 30 participants drawn from the three senatorial districts of Plateau. The initiative is to engender new methods of cattle production to the participants and as they acquire the skills, they are to transcend same to their individual communities in a bid encourage ranching as oppose to the open grazing methods being practiced by pastoralist on the Plateau.
