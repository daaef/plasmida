EDC/ ENTREPRENEURSHIP TRAININGS

Training and linkages services have also been a major feature of the Agency. On the 8th of June 2017 marked the official opening and signing of Memorandum of Understanding for the Central Bank Entrepreneurship Training in Jos, Plateau state. The Entrepreneurship Development Center of the CBN now has an office in Jos (PLASMIDA) and has so far trained over Two Hundred (200) participants who are currently waiting to be funded for their business plans with loans on a single digits interest rate payable in 3years.
