MSME FAIR IN JOS POLO

The Agency hosted the MSME Clinics in Jos Polo field between the 3rd and 4th of May 2017, having participants and top government officials present at the clinic. These amongst many included the Acting President, His Excellency, Prof. Yemi Osinbajo SAN, the Executive Governor Plateau state, His Excellency, Rt. Hon. Barr. Simon Bako Lalong, the Director General SMEDAN amongst others present. This led to the exhibition of a Tractor Fabricated in Jos and a Rice Processing plant, which where all sponsored by the Plateau State Microfinance Development Agency, linkages for businesses on the Plateau to relevant parties like FIRS, CAC, SON, ITF, and Bank of Industry among others present.
