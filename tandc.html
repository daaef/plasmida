Training and Consultancy

From the 27th February to 4th of March, 2017 featured the “Made in Plateau” Exhibition in collaboration with the GIZ. The event took place at the Crest Hotel, Jos. The Made in Plateau exhibition featured only products and commodities made here on the Plateau. It created a platform for the participants to network within the business community and they were also shown practically how to improve on their packaging and relevant linkages were established for them locally and internationally to sell their products and have relevant access to financing. Another initiative was the training embarked upon for Microfinance institutions and relevant bodies, this has been an integral part of activities the Agency has been embarked upon. The Agency has also promoted the deepening of financial inclusion by establishing a Microfinance Fund deliberately to support MSMEs in the State. Looking at the challenges women and youths face in accessing finance for business expansion, the establishment of the Microfinance Fund is to support MSME’s on the Plateau, further the setting up of the Artisans Job Centre in Jos where the skills of artisans can be strengthened for efficient service delivery and the establishment of the Fadama Microfinance Bank, being the first in Nigeria and West Africa has also been completed.

MSME’S DATA BANK

The development and deployment of a mobile application for data capturing of MSME’s on the Plateau. The Agency has succeeded in developing a data bank for MSME’s on the Plateau that can give you at a glance major information about these businesses, their capacity, location and Tax identification amongst other information that can be acquired. Which is a continuous exercise and ongoing for Micro Small and Medium Enterprises.
